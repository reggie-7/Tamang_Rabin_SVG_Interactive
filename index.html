BURPLE - Flavours

A Juicy Comeback
Burple was once a quirky 1990s juice brand known for its playful squeeze-bottle and bold flavor personality. Today, it’s back—reimagined with modern energy and nostalgic flair. The new Burple keeps the fun but updates the format, stepping into a fresh era with bold design, vibrant packaging, and flavors made to turn heads.
The Rebrand Vision This isn’t just a revival—it’s a reinvention. We’ve shifted from the original accordion-style bottles to new formats like cans and sleek bottles, giving Burple a more contemporary edge while keeping its signature whimsy. The design language mixes retro charm with bold graphics, drips, and bursts that feel alive and energetic.
Flavor Lineup
Our rebooted flavor range is built for maximum visual and taste impact:
1.Orange Zap – A citrusy burst that electrifies
2.Watermelon Rush – Juicy, sweet, and just a little wild
3.Grape Gush – Classic grape, dialed up to 11
4.Strawberry Splat – Berry-bright and drip-happy
5.Mango Pop – Tropical, sunny, and loud
6.Peach Fizz – Smooth, sweet, and slightly sparkling
Each flavor is crafted to embody Burple’s personality: bold, bouncy, and bursting with color and flavor.
